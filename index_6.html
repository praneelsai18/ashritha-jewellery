Return & Exchange Policy

At Ashritha Jewellers, we strive to provide you with the finest quality jewellery. Please read our policy carefully before initiating any request.

Important Notice We do not accept returns under any circumstances. However, if you receive a damaged product, we offer an exchange with valid proof. Please review the full policy below.

1. Eligibility for Exchange

We accept exchanges only under the following condition:

  • Damaged Product – If the product is received in a damaged condition due to shipping or manufacturing defects.

Returns Not Accepted

  • We do not accept returns for any reason.
  • Change of mind or personal dislike after purchase.
  • Custom-made, personalised, or engraved jewellery.
  • Minor colour variations due to screen differences.
  • Products damaged due to mishandling after delivery.

2. Exchange Request Process

To request an exchange, follow these steps within 24 hours of delivery:

1

Contact Us Immediately

Reach out via WhatsApp at +91 8688479975 within 24 hours of receiving your order.

2

Record an Unboxing Video

Provide a 360-degree uninterrupted unboxing video that clearly shows the shipping label, delivery address, and the damage. This is mandatory proof for all exchange requests.

3

Await Approval

Our team will review your request and respond with a decision within 24–48 business hours.

4

Ship the Product Back

If approved, ship the item in its original packaging with all tags and certificates intact, as per the instructions provided by our team.

Unboxing Video Requirement A 360-degree uninterrupted unboxing video including the shipping label and delivery address is mandatory. Requests without this proof will not be processed.

3. Exchange Shipping

  • Once your exchange request is approved, we will provide detailed shipping instructions.
  • Customers may need to self-ship the product in its original packaging with all tags and certificates intact.
  • In case of a seller error (damaged product), we will cover the return shipping costs.

4. Exchange Policy Conditions

  • If eligible, you can request an exchange for the same damaged item.
  • Exchanges are subject to product availability at the time of request.
  • If the same product is unavailable, we will offer an alternative of equal value.
  • No cash refund will be issued in any case.

5. Contact Us

For any exchange queries, reach out to us on WhatsApp — we're available to help you resolve any issues quickly and fairly.

Shipping Policy

We are committed to delivering your jewellery safely and promptly across India. Please read our shipping terms carefully.

1. Shipping Locations

  • We offer pan-India delivery to ensure our customers receive their favourite jewellery anywhere in the country.
  • For international shipping, please reach us on WhatsApp directly to discuss arrangements and charges.

2. Processing & Dispatch Time

Standard Order Timeline Once an order is confirmed and payment is received, it will be dispatched within 24 hours. After dispatch, your order will be delivered within 5–15 business days depending on your location.
  • Dispatch Time: Orders are dispatched within 24 hours of confirmation (standard products).
  • Orders placed on public holidays may be dispatched on the next working day.
  • Custom or personalised orders may take additional time — you will be informed separately.

3. Estimated Delivery Time

LocationEstimated Delivery
Metro Cities (Hyderabad, Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi, Chennai)5–7 Business Days
Tier 2 & Tier 3 Cities7–10 Business Days
Remote Areas & Villages10–15 Business Days

Delivery times may vary due to weather conditions, courier service delays, peak seasons, or other unforeseen circumstances.

4. Shipping Charges

  • Standard Shipping: A flat shipping charge of Rs 100 is applied to all orders.
  • Free Shipping: Applicable on orders above Rs 499 (as per current promotional offer).
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Additional COD handling charges may apply.
  • International Shipping: Charges are calculated based on destination and weight — contact us via WhatsApp for a quote.

5. Order Tracking

  • Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via WhatsApp or SMS.
  • You can track your order on the respective courier partner's website using the provided tracking number.
  • For any tracking issues, contact us on WhatsApp with your order ID and we will assist you.

6. Undelivered & Lost Shipments

  • If a package is lost in transit, we will assist in coordinating with the courier company to resolve the issue.
  • If an order is returned to us due to an incorrect address or non-availability of the recipient, re-shipping charges will apply.
  • Please ensure the delivery address and mobile number provided at checkout are accurate.

7. Damaged Shipments

If your order arrives damaged, you must inform us within 24 hours of delivery with an unboxing video as proof. Requests raised after 24 hours will not be entertained.
  • Record a 360-degree uninterrupted unboxing video showing the shipping label and the damage clearly.
  • We will assess the case and initiate a replacement as per our Return & Exchange Policy.
  • No cash refunds are offered for damaged shipments — only exchanges are possible.

8. Contact Us

For any queries regarding shipping, order tracking, or delivery — reach out to us on WhatsApp. We typically respond within a few hours.
