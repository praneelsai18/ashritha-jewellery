Return & Exchange Policy

At Ashritha Jewellers, we strive to provide you with the finest quality jewellery. Please read our policy carefully.

Important NoticeWe do not accept returns under any circumstances. However, if you receive a damaged product, we offer an exchange with valid proof.

1. Eligibility for Exchange

  • Damaged Product – If the product is received in a damaged condition due to shipping or manufacturing defects.

Returns Not Accepted

  • We do not accept returns for any reason.
  • Change of mind or personal dislike after purchase.
  • Custom-made, personalised, or engraved jewellery.
  • Minor colour variations due to screen differences.
  • Products damaged due to mishandling after delivery.

2. Exchange Request Process

1

Contact Us Immediately

Reach out via WhatsApp at +91 9381360636 within 24 hours of receiving your order.

2

Record an Unboxing Video

Provide a 360-degree uninterrupted unboxing video that clearly shows the shipping label, delivery address, and the damage.

3

Await Approval

Our team will review your request and respond within 24–48 business hours.

Unboxing Video RequirementA 360-degree uninterrupted unboxing video is mandatory. Requests without this proof will not be processed.

3. Contact Us

For any exchange queries, reach out to us on WhatsApp.

Rental Agreement — Please Read

✅The jewellery must be returned in the same condition as received — clean and undamaged.
✅A refundable security deposit is required at the time of booking. It will be returned after safe product return.
✅The rental period begins on the delivery date and ends on the agreed return date.
❌Any breakage, bending, stone loss, or major damage to the jewellery will be deducted from the security deposit.
❌The jewellery must NOT be worn during swimming, bathing, exercising, or any activity involving sweat or water.
❌Do not attempt to resize, repair, or alter the jewellery in any way.
❌Staining from perfume, lotion, or chemical contact is considered damage.
❌Late returns beyond 24 hours will incur additional rent charges per day.
❌Non-return of the item will result in full retail price being charged and legal action may be initiated.
